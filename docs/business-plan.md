# MaiHealth — Business Plan

## The Problem

Women between 25–33 with IBS (Reizdarm) are suffering. They're restricted in what they eat, where they go, how they plan their days. They've tried things — elimination diets, random supplements, maybe a gastroenterologist who told them "it's stress." Nothing has worked long-term because nobody has taken them through a structured, medically guided process.

## Target Client

- **Who**: Women, 25–33 years old
- **Condition**: IBS / Reizdarm — bloating, pain, food intolerances, unpredictable digestion
- **Mindset**: They're suffering, they're frustrated, they're ready to commit — but they need someone to guide them
- **Location**: Germany (remote via Zoom — no geographic limit)

## The Offer

### Step 1: Free Consultation (1 hour)

A no-cost, no-commitment video call. This is a two-way filter:

**For the client:**
- Understand the process
- See if Mai is the right fit
- Learn what commitment is required

**For Mai:**
- Assess whether the client is ready to stick to the routine
- Understand their symptoms, history, and goals
- Decide: *Is it worth going on this journey together?*

This is not a sales call. It's an honest conversation. If the client isn't ready to commit, Mai will tell them. If the case isn't a good fit, Mai will say so and refer elsewhere.

**Outcome**: Both sides decide yes or no. If yes → the client books the package.

---

### Step 2: The 12-Week Gut Health Program (Package)

Once both sides commit, the client books a structured package:

#### What's Included

| Component | Details |
|---|---|
| **Laboratory Testing** | Comprehensive gut diagnostics — stool analysis, microbiome, food intolerances, inflammation markers. Client pays the lab directly (separate cost from the package). |
| **9–12 Guided Sessions** | Video calls (Zoom) where Mai walks the client through the entire process step by step. From interpreting lab results to building a personalized nutrition and supplement plan. |
| **12 Weeks Duration** | The full program runs 12 weeks — enough time for real gut healing, not a quick fix. |
| **Ongoing Support** | Between sessions, the client has access to Mai for questions, check-ins, and accountability. Support channel TBD (WhatsApp, portal messaging, email). |

#### The Sessions (9–12 calls)

The program follows a clear clinical sequence — each phase builds on the previous one:

| Phase | What Happens | ~Timeline |
|---|---|---|
| **1. Consultation** | Free 1h call. Understand symptoms, history, lifestyle. Assess fit and commitment. | Before program starts |
| **2. Analysis** | Deep dive into the client's health picture. Detailed intake, symptom mapping, dietary assessment. Identify what to test. | Week 1 |
| **3. Lab Testing** | Order targeted diagnostics — stool analysis, microbiome, food intolerances, inflammation markers. Client pays lab directly. | Weeks 1–3 (waiting for results) |
| **4. Results & Plan** | Go through lab results together. Explain root causes. Build a personalized protocol — nutrition, elimination, lifestyle changes. | Week 3–4 |
| **5. Detox / Elimination** | Remove what's causing damage. Gut-irritating foods, identified triggers, inflammatory patterns. Clean the slate. | Weeks 4–6 |
| **6. Rebuild with Supplements** | Introduce targeted supplements — probiotics, prebiotics, gut-healing compounds. Repair the gut lining, rebalance the microbiome. | Weeks 6–9 |
| **7. Optimize & Reintroduce** | Fine-tune the protocol. Gradually reintroduce foods. Add further supplements as needed based on how the body responds. | Weeks 9–11 |
| **8. Stabilize & Graduate** | Confirm improvements. Build a long-term maintenance plan. Client knows their triggers and has the tools to manage independently. | Week 12 |

*(Phases overlap and timing varies by client — some need longer in detox, others move faster through reintroduction.)*

#### The Lab Work

- Ordered by Mai based on the client's symptoms
- Client pays the lab directly — this is a separate cost, not included in Mai's package fee
- Typical tests: comprehensive stool analysis, microbiome sequencing, food intolerance panels, inflammation markers (Calprotectin, Zonulin, etc.)
- Cost range: TBD (depends on which tests are needed)

## Key Differentiators

1. **Selective** — Mai doesn't take everyone. The free consultation is a filter. This builds trust and signals quality.
2. **Structured** — It's not "come back when you feel like it." It's a 12-week commitment with a clear path.
3. **Lab-based** — Not guessing. Real diagnostics, real data, personalized plan.
4. **Ongoing support** — Not just session-to-session. The client has someone in their corner for 12 weeks.
5. **Focused** — Only gut health / IBS. Mai is a specialist, not a generalist.
