## Section: Munich Practice (Longevity Praxis Munchen)

### Intro
A private practice offering holistic, functional medicine with state-of-the-art laboratory testing, personalized treatment plans, and long-term coaching. Available in-person in Munich/Grunwald and via nationwide telemedicine.

### Three Service Pathways

#### 1. Functional & Longevity Laboratory Testing
Comprehensive health analysis with extended blood work. Includes a personalized therapy plan covering nutrition, sleep, movement, and stress management, plus targeted micronutrient/supplement protocols and medications as needed.

#### 2. Analysis of Existing Lab Values
Review of current and previous lab results. One-on-one consultations to develop individualized health plans. Available in-person or virtually.

#### 3. Ongoing Health Management
Long-term, evidence-based health coaching. Monthly consultations tracking progress toward health goals. Available in Munich/Grunwald or via telemedicine nationwide.

### Clinical Focus Areas
- Cardiovascular disease prevention
- Women's health and fertility support
- Gut health optimization
- Autoimmune conditions and inflammation management
- Performance enhancement for executives and athletes
- Nutritional assessment and personalized dietary protocols
- Hormone balance evaluation
- Metabolic optimization

### Diagnostic Methods
- Advanced blood biomarker analysis
- Hormonal balance assessment
- Cardiovascular-metabolic health evaluation
- Nutritional status testing
- Genetic examinations (when appropriate)
- Inflammatory marker screening
- Detailed patient history collection

### Treatment Components
- Nutritional counseling
- Sleep optimization strategies
- Exercise programming
- Stress regulation techniques
- Targeted supplementation
- Pharmaceutical interventions (when medically necessary)

### Patient Journey (4 Phases)

#### Phase 1: Initial Consultation
Comprehensive patient history — in-person or telemedical.

#### Phase 2: Individualized Diagnostics
Testing tailored to the specific health situation.

#### Phase 3: Therapy & Prevention Plan
Structured, evidence-based plan development.

#### Phase 4: Ongoing Monitoring (Optional)
Progress tracking with plan adjustments.

### Four Foundational Pillars

#### Individualized Approach
Preventive medicine framework with detailed, personalized assessments.

#### Integrated Medicine
Systems-based understanding of interconnected bodily functions.

#### Personal Guidance
One-on-one counseling with evidence-based protocols.

#### Patient Commitment
Active investment and conscious participation in health management.

### Functional Medicine vs. Longevity Medicine
- **Functional Medicine** = the diagnostic and treatment methodology addressing root causes
- **Longevity Medicine** = the overarching objective of maximizing healthy lifespan through preventive strategies
- The practice combines both through comprehensive, causally-oriented interventions

### Insurance & Payment
- Not covered by German statutory health insurance (gesetzliche Krankenversicherung)
- Some private insurance plans may offer partial coverage depending on policy terms
- Most patients are self-paying
